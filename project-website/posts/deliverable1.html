Team Goals

Our main objective is to complete the assignment given by Professor Sohee Kang. The team will attempt to deliver a brand new, fully functional and aesthetically appealing version of WeBWork, an open source online homework system for math and statistic students. We will make sure our software will at least perform all the fundamental functions and meet the required standards.

Furthermore, we hope to create a design that is intuitive for different types of students, whether or not they’re experienced with technology. Also, we hope this learning experience will be a chance to challenge ourselves to develop better coding, teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skill. Ultimately, we wish to do our best to enrich the student’s overall learning experience.

Working Agreement

  1. When is your daily stand up?
    9:00 PM - On Slack chat
  2. What days will you be having this meeting?
    Every weekday, weekend meetings will take place as necessary.
  3. Where are you going to have this meeting?
    Slack/Facebook/Skype
  4. What is your preferred means of communication?
    Slack chat room/Facebook chat room
  5. How quickly should team members respond?
    Members must be present for meetings within 10 minutes. Any messages in the chat should be read within 24hrs, urgent messages should be sent through text to catch someone up.
  6. When should code be submitted?
    After each user story is completed.
  7. What is the team’s branching strategy?
    A branch for each major feature, merged only when tested.
  8. What will you do if a team member drops the course?
    Take over their part of the assignment and carry on.
  9. What are the expectations in terms of version control commits?
    In terms of messages on commits? Formatting for commit messages to personal branch should be understandable to the person working on the branch. Formatting for commit messages to the main branch should be as follows:

    Short description of what you’ve done (optional) Whatever.class.path.function
    Functionality added/changed
    Another.class.function
    Functionality added/changed
    Miscellaneous stuff at the end
  10. Who will review the submitted work to ensure that everything is there?
    Together in a meeting after we finish a sprint.
  11. Who is the Scrum master?
    J.R. Maingat
  12. How will we solve roadblocks in code, or in our team?
    Technical problem: Attempt to solve it with a fresh perspective on the problem, or talk about how to solve it at the end of daily meetings. Buggy Code/Insufficient documentation: Message the person who wrote the method, ask them to fix it.
